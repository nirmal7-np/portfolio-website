Threat Intelligence and its Role in SOC

Threat Intelligence is a process of collecting, analyzing, and disseminating information about current and emerging threats to an organization's security. In a Security Operations Center (SOC), threat intelligence plays a crucial role in proactively identifying and mitigating potential risks. By integrating threat intelligence into SOC operations, organizations can enhance their ability to detect, respond to, and prevent security incidents.
